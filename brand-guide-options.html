Lead with negative space. White and Cream should dominate layouts — giving the brand room to breathe. Lavender is the signature feature colour for cards, panels, and highlights. Charcoal and Periwinkle anchor text and accents. Olive is used sparingly as a contrast moment. Warm Grey supports captions and secondary text only.
